🥧 🍒 Delicious Strudel with Poppy Seeds, Walnuts, Apples, and Cherries 🍏 🌰

🔹 Dough Ingredients:

  • 💧 600 ml warm water
  • 🍞 40 g fresh yeast or 12 g dry yeast
  • 🍚 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 🧂 1 tablespoon salt
  • 🌾 1 kg fine flour
  • 🛢️ 70 + 20 + 20 ml oil for dough + 70 ml for greasing

🔹 Filling Ingredients:

Poppy Seed Filling:

  • 🌑 200 g ground poppy seeds
  • 🍚 150 g sugar
  • 🌾 2 tablespoons semolina
  • 🍯 3 tablespoons jam
  • 🥛 300 ml milk

Walnut Filling:

  • 🌰 200 g ground walnuts
  • 🍚 100 g sugar
  • 🍯 5 tablespoons jam
  • 🥛 100 ml milk

Apple Filling:

  • 🍏 4 large apples
  • 🍚 100 g sugar
  • 🌾 2 tablespoons semolina
  • 🍂 5 g cinnamon
  • 🍦 10 g vanilla sugar

Cherry Filling:

  • 🍒 400 g cherries
  • 🍚 150 g sugar
  • 🌾 3 + 5 tablespoons semolina (depending on cherry juice)

1️⃣ Preparing the Fillings

Poppy Seed Filling: Heat the milk, mix the ingredients, and obtain a creamy consistency.

Walnut Filling: Combine all ingredients and mix well.

Apple Filling: Grate the apples and mix with sugar, cinnamon, and semolina.

Cherry Filling: Mix cherries with sugar and semolina, adjusting the amount based on juice content.

2️⃣ Preparing the Dough

  1. Activate the yeast in warm water with sugar.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and knead until elastic dough forms.
  3. Let it rise for 30 minutes.

3️⃣ Forming and Baking the Strudel

  1. Divide the dough into 4 parts, let rest.
  2. Roll out each piece and spread the filling.
  3. Roll up and place on a baking tray.
  4. Brush with egg yolk and milk.
  5. Let rest for 30 minutes, then bake at 200°C for 55-60 minutes.

Note: If it browns too quickly, cover with baking paper.

😋 🍒 Enjoy! Bon Appetit! 🍰
